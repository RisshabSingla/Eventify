Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an event?

Once you're logged in, simply click on the "Create Event" button, fill in the event details, and publish your event.

How do I register for an event?

Browse through upcoming events, select one, and click on the "Register" button to sign up.

Can I cancel my event registration?

Yes, you can cancel your registration through the "My Events" section in your profile.

What payment options are available?

We currently support payments through PayPal, Stripe, and direct bank transfer for event registrations.
